Our Services

The Port of Spain City Corporation provides a wide range of services to maintain and enhance the city’s infrastructure and environment.

Provision and Maintenance of Corporation Buildings

We ensure that all Corporation buildings are well-maintained, secure, and functional to support the services offered to the public.

Construction and Maintenance of Drains and Watercourses

We handle the construction and maintenance of all drains and watercourses within the municipality, ensuring proper drainage and reducing flood risks.

Maintenance and Control of Parks and Public Spaces

We maintain and oversee parks, recreation grounds, and other public spaces, ensuring they are safe, clean, and accessible to all citizens.

Infrastructure Development

Our team focuses on infrastructure development within the Municipality, in accordance with plans approved by the Minister of Physical Planning.

Garbage Disposal

We provide efficient garbage disposal services from public and private properties to maintain a clean and healthy environment.

Chemical Treatment for Insect and Vector Control

We conduct chemical treatments to control insects and vectors, reducing health risks and improving the overall quality of life.

Public Nuisance Abatement and Primary Health Care

We work to abate public nuisances and provide primary health care services, ensuring the well-being of the city's residents.

Environmental Maintenance and Watercourse Monitoring

Our team is dedicated to maintaining the physical environment and monitoring watercourses to prevent environmental degradation.

Truck Borne Water Distribution

We manage the distribution of truck-borne water, ensuring that residents have access to clean water as per the Water and Sewage Act.

Enforcement of the Litter Act

We enforce the Litter Act through our Litter Prevention Wardens, helping to keep the city clean and litter-free.

Cesspit Cleaning

Our team provides cesspit cleaning services to ensure proper sanitation and prevent environmental contamination.

Supply of Food Badges

We issue food badges to vendors, ensuring they meet the required health and safety standards for food handling.

Market Maintenance and Control

We maintain and regulate the city’s markets, ensuring they are clean, safe, and conducive for business.

Maintenance of Burial Grounds and Crematorium

We are responsible for the upkeep and control of burial grounds and the crematorium, providing respectful and dignified services.
